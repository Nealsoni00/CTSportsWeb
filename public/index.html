CT Sports

A New Way to View Your High School Sports Schedules

HIGHLIGHTS

Features you love

Simple

Download the app and select your school and sports you or your children play. CT Sports automatically pulls the games from CIAC and handles the rest.

Track

Pull down to refresh the screen with all of the games and see updates. All information is up to date with your coach's schedule.

Reminders

Add reminders for games or add games to your apple calender. Ensures that you never miss a game.

  • 1
    Select Your School

    Download the app and you will be prompted to select your school. You can change this by pressing the settings button on the second page later.

  • 2
    Select Your Sports

    You will then be prompted to select what sports you (or your children) particiapte in. This will pull just your specific sports for the first page. You can change these sports by pressing the settings button on the first page later.

  • 3
    Pull to refresh

    Pull down on either your sports page or the school specific page to update the list of sporting events.

  • 4
    View Your Specific Sporting Events

    You can view the specific details of an event, including date, time, location, opponent, bus time and more, by tapping on the event on either page. You can also set reminders for events or even add them to your calender.

Sporting Events

Sporting events are taken live from the CIAC website. Each time you open the app or pull to refresh, they are pulled again from the website. This ensures that the data shown on your screen is as up to date as possible. Events are inputed by the coach or athletic director at your school.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the sports I participate in?

You can change the sports by simply tapping the settings icon on the first tab. This will show the sports that you particiapte in. You can then press "Add" to add a default sport or slide to remove a default sport.

Where do you get the sporting events from?

We get the sporting events from the CIAC website. They are inputed by your coach and will update when your coach or athletic director updates them

How do I change my school?

You can change the school by simply tapping the settings icon on the second page (your school's page). This will allow you to select the school and the sporting events from that school.

Why aren't all my events on the app?

This depends on the coach of your school. They would need to input the sporting events on the CIAC website itself.
